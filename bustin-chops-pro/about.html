Our Story

Crafting authentic South African biltong with passion and tradition

Who We Are

Bustin' Chops was born from a deep love for authentic South African flavours and a commitment to quality that never compromises. What started as a family tradition has grown into a passion project that brings the taste of home to biltong lovers everywhere.

Every batch of our biltong and droewors is handcrafted using traditional methods passed down through generations. We believe that great biltong isn't just about the meat – it's about patience, quality ingredients, and a whole lot of love.

Our Process

We source only the finest cuts of beef, carefully selected for marbling and quality. Each piece is hand-cut, marinated in our secret blend of traditional spices, and air-dried to perfection.

No shortcuts. No preservatives. Just pure, authentic biltong the way it was meant to be made. The result? Tender, flavourful biltong that melts in your mouth and keeps you coming back for more.
